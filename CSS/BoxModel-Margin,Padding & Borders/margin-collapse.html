i am box1 and my margin is set to 25px
i am box2 and i have a margin of 25px

In the above boxes, Box1 and Box2 both have a margin of 25px on all sides. However, the vertical margins between them collapse, so only a single 25px space is visible between Box1 and Box2 

VERY VERY IMPORTANT NOTE: 

Between box1 and box2:

box1 has margin-bottom: 25px
box2 has margin-top: 50px

BUT the browser collapses them into ONE margin.


Rule:
The larger vertical margin wins.
(Since 50px is larger than 25px, the visible gap is 50px.)
